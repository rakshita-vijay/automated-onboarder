Job Title: Microsoft Service Engineer
Job Summary: Ensure reliable operations of Microsoft's cloud infrastructure, supporting services like Office 365.

Responsibilities:
- Monitor and maintain service availability.
- Troubleshoot infrastructure issues in real-time.
- Implement automation scripts for efficiency.
- Coordinate with teams during incidents.
- Optimize systems for scalability.

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Engineering or IT.
- 2+ years in systems engineering.
- Knowledge of networking and cloud platforms.

Skills:
- Proficiency in scripting languages like PowerShell.
- Strong analytical mindset.
- On-call availability for emergencies.

Tech Stack:
- Languages: PowerShell, Python, Bash.
- Frameworks: Azure Monitor, Kubernetes.
- Tools: Docker, Terraform, Splunk.
