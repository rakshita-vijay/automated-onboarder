Job Title: Apple Chief Financial Officer
Job Summary: Handle Apple's financial strategy, planning, and reporting.

Responsibilities:
- Develop budgets and financial forecasts.
- Manage investments and capital allocation.
- Lead financial reporting and audits.

- 10+ years in financial leadership.
- Experience in public companies.

Skills:
- Financial modeling.
- Risk assessment.
- Regulatory knowledge.

Tech Stack:
- Tools: Excel, Oracle Financials, Tableau.
- Platforms: Custom financial reporting systems.
- Languages: VBA, SQL.
