The Overhaul: Strategy to Fix my Life
1) Plan out daily and medium/long term tasks using the Todo List app.  Use 
this thoroughly to ensure there are no task backlogs.
2) Practice strict NoFap.
3) Meditate for at least 15 minutes daily, preferably in the morning.
4) Study academic subjects for at least 2 hours everyday.
5) Do dedicated PS work for at least 45 minutes a day (on weekdays).
6) Go to bed by 11:30 pm and wake up by 7:30 am.
7) Do not waste time on social media during designated study hours.
